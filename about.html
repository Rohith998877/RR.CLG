About us

The AK innovative startup company was established in the year 2022, and sponsored by Sri SK AAKHIB innovative Society to promote technological products to meet the requirements of a developing nation in the context of global concern at the threshold of the twenty first century. A team of educated, enlightened, experienced technocrats with vision, This startup is an AI-First SaaS business aiming to become the leading voice automation and AI platform globally. The company provides Speech recognition and Voice assistants as a service to the banking, Food and Beverage and Hospitality industries. Their services have the least human interference and can handle complex servicing issues as well. Founded in 2016, the company has raised $5.1 million in funding in the year 2020.
